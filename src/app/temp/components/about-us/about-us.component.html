The Ministry of Health Zanzibar

The Ministry of Health Zanzibar is responsible for overseeing all health-related matters in Zanzibar. In the early 1990s, the Ministry initiated a comprehensive reform process in 2002. This effort led to the development of the Strategic Plan for the Health Sector (ZHSRSP I) 2002/03–2006/07, followed by ZHSRSP II 2006/07–2010/11, which was based on the 1999 Zanzibar Health Policy.

Vision

To ensure access to reliable health services for all people in Zanzibar, promoting equality.

Mission

To provide strategic leadership within the Ministry of Health in Zanzibar, ensuring that all Zanzibaris uphold their right to quality and equitable healthcare services.
