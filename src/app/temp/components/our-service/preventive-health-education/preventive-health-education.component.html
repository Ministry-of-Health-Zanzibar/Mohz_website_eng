Preventive and Health Education

The provision of preventive services and health education includes maternal and child health services, HIV/AIDS control services, Tuberculosis and Leprosy, Malaria elimination services in Zanzibar, and nutrition services.

Education and Epidemics

Provide education on epidemics such as cholera and Ebola to the relevant areas and alert the general public on how to protect themselves from these diseases.

Health and Environmental Education

Health education encompasses Environmental Health education, disease behavior monitoring, non-communicable diseases, and Neglected Tropical Diseases (NTDs). Additional services include:

  • Primary mental health services
  • Primary eyesight services
  • Health services at Harbor and Airport
