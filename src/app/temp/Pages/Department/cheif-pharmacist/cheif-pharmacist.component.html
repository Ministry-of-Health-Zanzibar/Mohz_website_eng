Chief Pharmacist Office (CPO)

A department under the Ministry of Health, Zanzibar responsible for governance, regulation, and coordination of pharmaceutical services.

Overview

The Chief Pharmacist Office (CPO) was established after the Zanzibar Revolution of 1964 and officially recognized as a department in 2010.

It oversees pharmaceutical services across Zanzibar, ensuring the availability and rational use of medicines.

Core Values

Integrity
Professionalism
Accountability
Equity & Innovation

Vision

All people living in Zanzibar have access to safe, efficacious, and quality essential medicines that are affordable.

Mission

To ensure sustainable access to affordable essential medicines by strengthening governance, coordination, and rational use of pharmaceutical services in Zanzibar.

Objectives

General Objective

To strengthen pharmaceutical systems in Zanzibar to ensure availability and rational use of affordable medicines.

Specific Objectives

  • Develop, review, and implement pharmaceutical policies, guidelines, and standards
  • Strengthen governance, leadership, and coordination of pharmaceutical services
  • Improve planning and management of pharmaceutical supply systems
  • Promote rational use of medicines in line with national policies
  • Enhance capacity of pharmaceutical personnel and monitoring systems
