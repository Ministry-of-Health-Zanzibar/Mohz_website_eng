Chief Pharmacist Office (CPO) & LMU

Organizing and supporting Zanzibar's health commodity supply chain.

visibility Vision

To be a center of excellence for supply chain management of health commodities in sub-Saharan Africa by 2020.

flag Mission

To ensure availability and accessibility of quality medical supplies through efficient logistics supported by technology.

assignment Functions of LMU

The LMU’s functions are both strategic and operational:

  • Strategic: Prioritizing supply chain, coordinating stakeholders, and securing resources.
  • Operational: Managing logistics, enhancing data visibility, and ensuring efficient supply chains.

important_devices Importance of LMU

Ensures health commodities are available when and where needed, supporting essential healthcare services.

history History & Collaboration

Operational since 2016, LMU evolved to SCMU after recognizing broader responsibilities including policy, quality, and resource planning.

The LMU is a critical backbone in Zanzibar’s health system, ensuring timely availability of quality medical supplies.
