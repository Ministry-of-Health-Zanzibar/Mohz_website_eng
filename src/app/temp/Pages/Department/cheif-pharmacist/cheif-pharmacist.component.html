Chief Government Chemist Laboratory (CGCL)

Serving Zanzibar for over a century with scientific precision and commitment to public health.

Overview

CGCL started as a small unit and evolved into a key laboratory agency under the Laboratory Act No. 10 of 2011.

It supports national systems in forensic, environmental, chemical, food, and drug analysis with quality assurance.

CGCL Divisions

Forensic Analysis

Conducts toxicological and forensic tests supporting justice and security.

Chemical Analysis

Analyzes industrial and imported chemicals to ensure public safety.

Environmental Testing

Monitors air, water, and soil quality to protect the ecosystem.

Food & Drug Lab

Ensures food and drug products meet safety standards.

Future Plans

To become a regional scientific leader with expanded facilities, expert personnel, and advanced research capabilities.
