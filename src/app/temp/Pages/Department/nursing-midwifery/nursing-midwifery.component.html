Directorate of Nursing and Midwifery

Founded in 2023 under President Dr. Hussein Ali Mwinyi, committed to transforming nursing and midwifery services for better healthcare.

Introduction

The Directorate, launched on January 30, 2023, in Zanzibar, addresses critical healthcare needs, especially for women, children, and the elderly.

Key Divisions:
Division of Nursing and Midwifery:

Ethics, client care, general oversight.

Pemba Nursing Division:

All services specific to Pemba Island.

Standards & Research Division:

Training, research, and publication.

Main Functions

✅ Improve nursing and midwifery care quality across sectors.

✅ Offer strategic guidance to government on nursing policy.

✅ Promote standardized, evidence-based practices.

✅ Coordinate continuous education and development.

✅ Foster local and international partnerships.

Our Mission

Deliver compassionate, high-quality nursing and midwifery services that uphold human dignity and global standards.

Our Vision

Empower nurses and midwives to deliver competent, respectful, and quality healthcare services with empathy and professionalism.
