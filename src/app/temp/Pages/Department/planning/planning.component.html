Department of Planning, Policy and Research

A vital unit of the Ministry of Health Zanzibar, driving strategic health improvements through structured planning, research, and policy development.

Overview

This department formulates strategic plans, evaluates policy implementation, and steers research to improve health systems across Zanzibar.

It plays a crucial role in aligning health sector plans with national development goals, ensuring sustainable healthcare improvements.

Core Services

Planning & Coordination

Oversees health sector planning to align with national development strategies and healthcare goals.

Monitoring & Evaluation

Tracks implementation of health policies and ensures that strategic targets are being met effectively.

Policy Development

Drafts, reviews, and refines health policies to ensure they are relevant, practical, and impactful.

Health Research

Conducts research to generate data for evidence-based decision making and continuous health improvement.

Stakeholder Engagement

Collaborates with partners, NGOs, and government bodies to ensure effective implementation of plans.
