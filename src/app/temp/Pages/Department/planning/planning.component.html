Department of Planning, Policy and Research

The Department of Planning, Policy, and Research is a crucial arm of the Ministry of Health Zanzibar, dedicated to ensuring effective planning and policy formulation to improve health outcomes.

Services

  • Managing units and directing all departmental responsibilities.
  • Ensuring that all departmental requirements are prepared and managed in a timely manner in line with government directives and stakeholder feedback.
  • Overseeing the preparation of the annual plan and the strategic plan for the ministry's mid-term development.
