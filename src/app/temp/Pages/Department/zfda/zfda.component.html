Zanzibar Food and Drug Agency (ZFDA)

Established in 2007, the Zanzibar Food and Drug Agency (ZFDA) operates under the Zanzibar Food, Drugs, and Cosmetics Act # 2 of 2006, reviewed in 2017, with the objective of ensuring the safety and quality of food, drugs, cosmetics, medical devices, and related products in Zanzibar.

Core Regulatory Functions

  • Products Inspection
  • Registration
  • Quality Control

Public Awareness

ZFDA strives to raise public awareness regarding the safety and quality of the regulated products through various mass media.

Laboratory Services

The Board has strengthened laboratory services by equipping the laboratory with basic equipment, reducing sample analysis durations. A new microbiology laboratory has also been installed.

Challenges

Despite notable achievements, ZFDA faces challenges such as inadequate resources and limited cooperation from stakeholders and law enforcers.

Acknowledgements

ZFDA appreciates the commitment of its staff and collaborative partners in protecting and promoting public health through the implementation of the ZFDA Act # 2 of 2006 and its 2017 review. Special thanks go to development partners like WHO, UNICEF, FAO, and GF.
