Established in 2007, the Zanzibar Food and Drug Agency (ZFDA) operates under the Zanzibar Food, Drugs, and Cosmetics Act #2 of 2006 (reviewed in 2017), with the objective of ensuring the safety and quality of food, drugs, cosmetics, medical devices, and related products in Zanzibar.

Core Regulatory Functions
  • Products Inspection
  • Registration
  • Quality Control
Public Awareness

ZFDA raises public awareness through mass media on safety and quality of regulated products.

Laboratory Services

Equipped laboratories reduce sample analysis times, with new microbiology labs added.

Challenges

Limited resources and stakeholder cooperation remain key implementation challenges.
