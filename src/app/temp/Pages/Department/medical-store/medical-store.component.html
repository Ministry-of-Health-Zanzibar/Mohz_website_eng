Medical Store - Zanzibar

Promoting health care delivery through access to essential medicines and quality medical supplies.

Overview

Upgraded to a Department in 2011, the Medical Store operates warehouses in Maruhubi (Unguja) and Micheweni (Pemba).

It manages the receipt, storage, and distribution of essential medicines, devices, and diagnostic equipment supporting government health decisions.

Medical Store Divisions

Customer Service

Ensures customer satisfaction and handles inquiries efficiently.

Quality Control

Maintains the quality standards of medicines and supplies.

Procurement & Management

Oversees procurement and medication stock control.

Admin & Finance

Handles financial operations and administration.

Services Provided

Receiving Medicines & Supplies

Responsible for accepting medical stock from the government and partners.

Storage & Handling

Supervises the proper storage and handling of medicines and supplies.

Distribution

Distributes medicines and supplies to 190+ health centers across Unguja and Pemba.

Supervision

Supervises proper use of medicines and supplies at health facilities.

Training & Guidance

Trains pharmacists and storekeepers on proper storage and handling.
