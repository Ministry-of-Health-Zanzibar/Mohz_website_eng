Administration and HR - Ministry of Health Zanzibar

The objectives of the Administration and HR department are to enable the Department and its units to discharge their responsibilities effectively by maintaining a favorable working environment. We ensure the availability of sufficient resources to enable employees to fulfill their responsibilities.

Objectives of the Administration and HR Department

  • Work Environment: Maintain a favorable working environment to enhance productivity.
  • Staffing: Address staffing issues such as recruitment, promotions, and certification of employees.
  • Staff Capacity Building: Provide short and long-term training to help staff gain necessary credentials and skills.
  • Contractual Issues: Manage personnel contractual issues (OPRAS) and other administrative matters.
  • Confidentiality: Maintain confidentiality of the Ministry's documents and personal information of staff members.
  • Retirement Services: Serve retired national leaders according to the benefits law (Act No. 3 of 1999).

Services Provided by the Administration and HR Department

  • Employment and Recruitment: Facilitate the recruitment of new employees and manage promotions.
  • Capacity Building: Provide training programs to enhance employee skills and qualifications.
  • Staff Confidentiality: Maintain confidential information regarding staff members.
  • Coordination of Personnel Matters: Coordinate contractual issues, benefits, and related HR policies.
  • Retirement Benefits: Administer benefits for retired national leaders in compliance with the law.
