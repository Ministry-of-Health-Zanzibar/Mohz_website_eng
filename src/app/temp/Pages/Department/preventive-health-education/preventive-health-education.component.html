Preventive and Health Education

The department provides essential preventive services and health education, focusing on maternal and child health, HIV/AIDS control, tuberculosis and leprosy, malaria elimination, and nutrition services in Zanzibar.

Preventive Services

  • Maternal and Child Health Services
  • HIV/AIDS Control Services
  • Tuberculosis and Leprosy Services
  • Malaria Elimination Services
  • Nutrition Services

Health Education

Education on epidemics such as cholera and Ebola is provided to relevant areas, alerting the public on how to prevent these diseases.

Health education also covers:

  • Environmental Health Education
  • Disease Behavior Education
  • Monitoring of Staff's Health
  • Non-communicable Diseases
  • Neglected Tropical Diseases (NTDs)
  • Primary Mental Health Services
  • Primary Eyesight and Health Services at Harbor and Airport
