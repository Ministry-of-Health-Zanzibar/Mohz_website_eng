Curretive Services in Zanzibar

This section highlights the key boards, responsibilities, and services provided by the Department of Curretive to enhance healthcare in Zanzibar.

Boards

The Department of Curretive comprises various Boards such as:

  • Physicians Board
  • Private Hospital Advisory Board
  • Community Health and Environmental Board
  • Government Chemistry General Agency Board
  • Food and Drug Administration Board
  • Mental Health, Nursing, and Midwives Council
  • Office of the Chief Nurse

The department is also responsible for upgrading Village Hospitals to District Hospitals and District Hospitals to Regional Hospitals.

Services Provided

  • Control of Chemicals, Food, Drugs, and Cosmetics.
  • Laboratory and Radiation Examination Services.
  • Medical services that include access, care, and distribution of medicines.
  • Safe Blood Program Services.

Safe Blood Program

In ensuring safe blood supply, the Ministry, through the Safe Blood Unit, continues to collect, monitor, store, and distribute blood in different hospitals across the country. The Ministry, in collaboration with other institutions, conducts blood donation drives to ensure an adequate blood supply in all hospitals nationwide.
