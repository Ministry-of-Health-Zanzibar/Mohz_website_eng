Curative Services in Zanzibar

A comprehensive overview of boards, responsibilities, and services that elevate healthcare delivery across Zanzibar.

Overview of Boards

The Department of Curative comprises several critical boards that oversee and support the delivery of healthcare services.

Key Boards

Physicians Board

Oversees medical practitioners and clinical standards.

Private Hospital Advisory Board

Guides private healthcare facilities on compliance and improvements.

Community Health & Environmental Board

Monitors community health and environmental safety.

Chemistry General Agency Board

Handles standards related to chemical and laboratory services.

Food & Drug Administration Board

Regulates food safety and pharmaceutical compliance.

Mental Health & Nursing Council

Supervises mental health and nursing professionals.

Services Provided

Chemical & Drug Control

Regulates chemicals, foods, drugs, and cosmetics safety.

Lab & Radiation Services

Provides diagnostic testing and radiation assessments.

Medical Care Access

Ensures medical services and medicine distribution.

Safe Blood Program

Maintains blood safety through collection and distribution.

The Department of Curative remains committed to sustainable, accessible, and equitable healthcare for all citizens of Zanzibar.
