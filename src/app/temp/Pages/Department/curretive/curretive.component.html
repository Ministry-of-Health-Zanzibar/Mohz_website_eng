Curative Services in Zanzibar

A comprehensive overview of boards, responsibilities, and services that elevate healthcare delivery across Zanzibar.

Boards

The Department of Curative comprises several key boards:

  • Physicians Board
  • Private Hospital Advisory Board
  • Community Health and Environmental Board
  • Government Chemistry General Agency Board
  • Food and Drug Administration Board
  • Mental Health, Nursing, and Midwives Council
  • Office of the Chief Nurse

The department also upgrades Village Hospitals to District and Regional levels.

Services Provided

  • Control of Chemicals, Food, Drugs, and Cosmetics
  • Laboratory and Radiation Examination Services
  • Medical services including access, care, and medicine distribution
  • Safe Blood Program Services
