Alternative Medicine - Zanzibar

Highlighting the vision, mission, goal, and policies guiding the integration of Traditional and Alternative Medicine (TAM) into Zanzibar’s healthcare system.

Overview

Traditional and Alternative Medicine (TAM) is being integrated into Zanzibar’s healthcare system to complement modern medical practices and ensure accessible, quality healthcare for all.

This involves conserving indigenous knowledge, regulating practice, and promoting sustainable use of medicinal resources.

Vision, Mission, Goals & Policies

Vision

Full integration of traditional medicine into the national health system to complement modern medicine and ensure better health for all.

Mission

Promote traditional medicine and enhance household incomes via conservation, cultivation, harvesting, and trade in medicinal resources, while contributing to national growth.

Goal

Regulate TAM practice, protect indigenous knowledge, and improve TAM quality, efficacy, and accessibility for better community health.

Policies

Establish official TAM guidelines, encourage sustainable resource use, protect indigenous knowledge, and integrate TAM into the healthcare system.

Promote collaboration among stakeholders to ensure a sustainable and regulated TAM ecosystem for the benefit of all Zanzibaris.

Let us ensure the sustainable growth of Traditional and Alternative Medicine in Zanzibar for a healthier tomorrow.
