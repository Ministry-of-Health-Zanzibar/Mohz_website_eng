Alternative Medicine in Zanzibar

This component highlights the vision, mission, goal, and policies guiding the integration of Traditional and Alternative Medicine (TAM) into Zanzibar’s healthcare system.

Vision

The vision is the full integration of traditional medicine practice into the national health system to complement modern medicine, ensuring better health for all.

Mission

The mission is to promote traditional medicine and its integration into the national healthcare system; alleviate poverty by enhancing household incomes through promoting conservation, cultivation, harvesting, and trade in medicinal plants and other medicinal resources. Additionally, contributing to national economic growth through registration, licensing, and other revenue collection measures.

Goal

The overall goal is to guide and regulate TAM practice while protecting indigenous knowledge, intellectual property, consumer and other rights, and medicinal resources. This aims to improve the quality, efficacy, and use of TAM and raise the capability and efficiency of TAM practitioners for the better health of the community.

Policies

Traditional and Alternative Medicine (TAM) has been practiced in Zanzibar for years without official guidelines. However, there has been growing public awareness about the potential benefits of TAM to the health sector. With the formulation of this policy, the Ministry of Health aims to guide TAM practice, enhance its benefits, and ensure sustainable use of resources. The policy also outlines the role of TAM in Zanzibar’s healthcare system and establishes a framework for the development and regulation of TAM practices.

The policy encourages cooperation between stakeholders to ensure the proper use of natural resources, the protection of indigenous knowledge, and the promotion of sustainable environmental practices. By integrating TAM into the national healthcare system, Zanzibar aims to offer better healthcare and improve the overall health of its people.

It is hoped that all stakeholders will take responsibility to ensure the growth and sustainable practice of Traditional and Alternative Medicine in Zanzibar for a healthier future.
