Mnazi Mmoja Hospital - Zanzibar

Providing quality, accessible, and reliable healthcare services to all Zanzibaris with dedication and excellence.

Overview

Mnazi Mmoja Hospital, located in Stone Town, Zanzibar, is the main referral and teaching hospital under the Ministry of Health.

It offers a wide range of clinical and diagnostic services, supporting both primary and tertiary healthcare across the islands.

Hospital Departments

Emergency

24/7 emergency care services for urgent medical needs.

Surgery

Operates specialized surgical units and operation theaters.

Maternity & Child Health

Comprehensive maternal and child healthcare services.

Laboratory & Imaging

Diagnostic services including blood tests, X-rays, and scans.

Services Offered

Outpatient Services

Regular health checkups and consultations across departments.

Inpatient Care

Full admission and continuous care for critical patients.

Pharmacy Services

Access to prescribed medications and drug consultation.

Specialist Clinics

Specialist care in cardiology, dermatology, ENT, and more.
