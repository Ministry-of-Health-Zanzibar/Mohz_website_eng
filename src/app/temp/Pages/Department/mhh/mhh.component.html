Zanzibar's Premier Referral and Tertiary Hospital

Overview

MMH is a semi-autonomous public hospital established by Act No 3 of 2016 with 678 beds across three campuses:

  • Main Campus: 541 beds, Stone Town
  • Mwembeladu Maternity: 37 beds
  • Kidongo Chekundu: 100 beds, mental care

Management

MMH operates through 8 Directorates:

  • Planning & Finance
  • Operations & Administration
  • Clinical Services
  • Nursing, Pharmacy, Engineering
  • Training, Research, Diagnostics

Clinical Departments

20 departments and units including:

  • Medicine, Pediatrics, Surgery
  • Obstetrics & Gynecology
  • ENT, Dentistry, Ophthalmology
  • Radiology, Lab Services

Specialty Clinics

  • Gynecology, Surgical Clinics, ENT
  • Acupuncture, Physical Therapy
  • Diabetes, Hypertension Clinics
  • HIV/STD Clinics

Collaborative Services

Partnering with national programs for:

  • TB & Leprosy Clinics
  • Methadone Therapy
  • HIV Care & CTC Clinics
