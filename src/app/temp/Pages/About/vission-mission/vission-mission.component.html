Vision and Mission

Vision

Access to reliable health services for all Zanzibar and equality.

Mission

Provide strategic leadership of the Ministry of Health in Zanzibar which will ensure that all Zanzibaris protect their right to quality and equitable health care services.

Health Administration Policies

Purpose of the Policy: To promote inclusivity, transparency, accountability, community involvement, and participation in decision-making on health issues at all levels.

  • Provision of Health Care: To improve referral systems within health settings at every level including the private health sector.
  • Social Services: To improve the integration and management of social services among different actors at each level.
  • Human Resources for Health: To increase the number of skilled workers at all levels of the healthcare system.
  • Infrastructure: To develop and adhere to an infrastructure development program that promotes equity and sustainability in maintenance and rehabilitation services.
  • Essential Medicines, Medical and Non-medical Equipment: To increase access to quality medicines, medical and non-medical equipment.
  • Health Laws and Regulations: To promote the application of health laws, rules, and standards of conduct in health-related matters.
  • Health Information: To promote the establishment of a Health Information Management system for evidence-based decisions.
  • Creativity and Research: To promote research activities in the ministry.
  • Health Funding: To maximize financial resources by adopting a variety of health and financing options that are sustainable.
  • Cross-cutting Theme: To promote gender and human rights approaches in the healthcare system and improve environmental management and waste management in healthcare settings.
