Zanzibar Malaria Elimination Programme (ZAMEP)

ZAMEP plays a vital role in controlling malaria through strategic interventions aligned with national health goals.

Strategic Alignment

ZAMEP aligns with the following national frameworks:

  • MKUZA II – National Strategy for Growth and Reduction of Poverty
  • Zanzibar National Health Policy (2009)
  • Health Sector Strategic Plan II (2006/07–2010/11)

This alignment ensures global malaria elimination goals are met.

Key Responsibilities

  • Design and implement malaria control interventions
  • Evaluate program effectiveness and impact
  • Coordinate with all health system stakeholders
  • Foster collaboration for health system strengthening

Health System Strengthening (HSS)

ZAMEP ensures effective coordination, mobilization, and capacity building for malaria control to enhance public health in Zanzibar.
