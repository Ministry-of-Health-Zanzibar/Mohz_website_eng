Integrated Reproductive and Child Health (IRCH)

The IRCH Unit aims to improve maternity and child health, reducing maternal and child mortality. It contributes directly to MDGs, MKUZA III, and Vision 2020.

Key Units

  • Reproductive and Child Health Unit (RCH)
  • Expanded Program Immunization Unit (EPI)
  • Integrated Management of Childhood Illness Unit (IMCI)

There are 172 Health Centers across Unguja and Pemba providing reproductive and child health services.

Immunization Services

  • Distribution of new vaccine refrigerators
  • Ongoing immunization at all centers
  • Supervised vaccine transport & distribution
  • Bi-annual vaccination activity evaluations
  • Vaccine surveillance and district monitoring
  • Collaborative measles vaccination campaigns
  • Strengthening vaccine immunity
  • Pediatric Services for Equality

Maternal & Infant Mortality Evaluation

The IRCH evaluates the causes of maternal and infant mortality in Unguja and Pemba to improve healthcare quality and outcomes.
