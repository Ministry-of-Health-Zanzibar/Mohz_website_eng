Integrated Reproductive and Child Health (IRCH)

The Integrated Reproductive and Child Health (IRCH) Unit aims to improve maternity and child health and reduce maternal and child mortality. Its activities contribute directly to achieving the Millennium Development Goals, MKUZA III, and Vision 2020.

Key Units

  • Reproductive and Child Health Unit (RCH)
  • Expanded Program Immunization Unit (EPI)
  • Integrated Management of Childhood Illness Unit (IMCI)

There are 172 Health Centers across Unguja and Pemba providing coverage services for reproductive and child health.

Immunization Services

  • Distribution of new vaccine refrigerators to Health Centers
  • Continued immunization services at all reproductive and child health centers
  • Supervision of vaccine transportation and distribution
  • Evaluation of vaccination activities every six months
  • Immunization surveillance for vaccines
  • Monitoring vaccination activities in districts and health centers
  • Collaborative measles vaccination campaigns
  • Strengthening immunity against vaccines
  • Pediatric Services for Equality

Maternal and Infant Mortality Evaluation

The IRCH evaluates the causes of maternal and infant mortality in Unguja and Pemba regions to improve the quality of care and reduce mortality rates.
